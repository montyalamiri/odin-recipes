Pasta al Limone

Description

Pasta al Limone is a really good pasta dish. Anyone who does not like it either sucks at cooking or has no taste at all!

Ingredients

  • 1 Lemon
  • 12 oz. Spaghetti
  • Kosher Salt
  • 3⁄4 cup Heavy Cream
  • 6 tbsp. Unsalted Butter
  • 3 oz. Finely Grated Parmesan (about 3⁄4 cup)
  • Freshly Ground Black Pepper.
